Skybase ✨

Ship projects, get prizes, repeat!

How this works?

Skybase is a 3 week program where you are challenged to ship four unique projects ranging from Game and Web Development, CAD, and even Hardware. These genres come in the form of islands that unlock every fifth day, giving you a short but not too long range of five days to complete and ship each of your projects successfully! By completing each project you earn charms that you can redeem for stickers, swag and more!
