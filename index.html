Skybase ✨


How does this works?

Skybase is an intensive three-week program that challenges you to ship four unique projects across Game Dev, Web Dev, CAD, and Hardware. These categories are presented as "floating islands" that unlock every five days. You’ll need to work fast as you only have five days to complete the project—once a new island drops, the previous one closes for good. Successfully shipping your projects earns you Charms, which can be redeemed for exclusive stickers, swag, and more.

The islands that unlock every five days, reveals a new genre that rotates around:

  • Game Development
  • CAD
  • Web Development, and
  • Hardware!

Don't know how to code?

Don't worry! We've got you covered, every island that unlocks will contain resources that will guide you through building your project step-by-step start to finish, so you can easily hop right in. Still feel stuck? feel free to reach out to people in the channel, the community loves to help out innovative people who are just starting to code!

What if I miss an island?

After an island closes—it is gone for good, meaning that you cannot submit your project for it anymore. Each island remains open for a period of five days, so try to participate frequently to maximize your earnings and rewards!
